About me

👋 Hi, I'm Chenyi Zhang, a linguist.

👀 I'm interested in neurolinguistics, psycholinguistics, multilingualism, language and education.

✨ I did my PhD at Department of Theoretical and Applied Linguistics, University of Cambridge, under the supervision of Prof. Ianthi Maria Tsimpli.

📚 You can read my PhD thesis here (and don't let me know if there is any typos!): Text-Picture Integration during Inferential Processing by L1 and L2 English and Chinese Speakers: Individual Differences and Script Effects

🌱 I'm currently the Research Laboratory Manager at Faculty of Modern and Medieval Languages and Linguistics, University of Cambridge. I "manage" two labs: Cambridge Phonetics Laboratory (CamPAL), and the Phonetics Lab.

🎯 If you are interested in participating in linguistic experiments and contribute to linguistic research, please check out our SONA page!

💖 I'm open to collaboration!

📧 How to reach me: cz344 [at] cam [dot] ac [dot] uk. I am always open to collaboration!

😊 Pronouns: She/her/hers

⚡ Fun fact: The Chinese characters for my name are 晨怡, meaning galaxy, time, and happiness.
